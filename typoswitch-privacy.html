Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  TypoSwitch Chrome Extension  ·  Developer: Lital Entin  ·  lital.entin@gmail.com

TL;DR — The short version

  • We do not collect any of your data
  • We do not store any text you type or fix
  • We do not send anything to any server
  • Everything runs locally in your browser
  • No account required, no tracking, no ads

1. Overview

TypoSwitch ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that converts mistyped text between Hebrew and English keyboard layouts. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension accesses and how it is handled.

We are committed to your privacy. TypoSwitch is designed to work entirely locally — no data ever leaves your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

TypoSwitch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Text you type or select in your browser
  • Websites you visit or URLs you browse
  • Personal information of any kind
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Keyboard input or clipboard content
  • Browsing history

3. How the Extension Works

TypoSwitch operates entirely within your browser using local JavaScript processing:

  • When you trigger a fix (via shortcut, popup, or right-click), the extension reads the selected text or last typed word directly from the active text field
  • The text is converted locally using a character mapping algorithm
  • The converted text is placed back into the text field
  • No data is sent to any external server at any point
All text processing happens 100% locally inside your browser. TypoSwitch has no backend server and makes no network requests.

4. Permissions Explained

TypoSwitch requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — Used only to save your preferences (e.g. whether to show toast notifications). No personal data is stored.
  • contextMenus — Used to add the "Fix keyboard layout" option to your right-click menu.
  • host_permissions (all_urls) — Required to allow the extension to interact with text fields on any website you visit. This does NOT mean we read or collect your browsing data.

5. Local Storage

TypoSwitch uses Chrome's local storage API exclusively to save your extension preferences (such as whether toast notifications are enabled). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Contains no personal information
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension

6. Compatibility Note

TypoSwitch works on most websites and text fields across the web. However, some websites (such as Google Search and other Google products) restrict direct text manipulation by browser extensions for their own security reasons. This is a browser-level limitation and is not related to TypoSwitch. No data is collected in these cases either.

7. Third-Party Services

TypoSwitch does not integrate with, connect to, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

8. Children's Privacy

TypoSwitch does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no special risk to children's privacy.
